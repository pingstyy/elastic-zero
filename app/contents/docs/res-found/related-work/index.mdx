# Related Work

A selection of the most influential papers and models in the context of scalable, RLVR-optimized and self-improving reasoning systems:

## Chain-of-Thought, RLVR, and Length Control
- *Chain-of-Thought Prompting*: J. Wei et al., 2022, arXiv:2201.11903
- *Self-Consistency for CoT*: X. Wang et al., 2023, arXiv:2203.11171
- *S1, L1, O1*: Aggarwal & Welleck (2025), Muennighoff et al. (2025), OpenAI o1 System Card (2024)
- *DeepSeekR1, DeepScaleR*: Guo et al., (2025); Luo et al. (2025)
- *Budget Conscious Reasoning*: Yuan et al., 2024
- *C3oT*: Kang et al., 2024, arXiv:2412.11664

## Self-Play, Curriculum, and RL with Verifiable Rewards
- *AlphaGo, AlphaZero*: Silver et al., 2016, 2017
- *STaR*: Zelikman et al., NeurIPS 2022
- *SPIN, Self-Rewarding LMs, SPC, SPAG*: Chen et al., 2024; Yuan et al., 2024
- *Minimo*: Poesia et al., 2024 (NeurIPS)
- *DeepSeek Prover v2*: Ren et al., 2025

## Zero Setting and Absolute Zero Reasoning
- *Absolute Zero*: Zhao et al., (2025, arXiv:2505.03335)
- *SimpleRL-Zoo, Oat-Zero, Open-Reasoner-Zero*: Liu et al., 2025; Hu et al., 2025

## Code Reasoning, Datasets & Execution
- *LiveCodeBench*: Jain et al., 2024
- *Self-improving Math*: Min et al., 2024
- *TACO*: Li et al., 2023
